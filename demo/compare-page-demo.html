Expert Insight

The verdict depends entirely on your spending habits.

If you spend $600+ monthly on groceries and dining, the Amex Cobalt will deliver significantly more value. Its 5x multiplier is unmatched in Canada, and you'll easily offset the annual fee with everyday purchases.

However, if you're a frequent Air Canada traveler or value comprehensive travel insurance, the TD Aeroplan Visa Infinite is the smarter choice. The 50,000-point welcome bonus alone is worth $500-750 in flights, and the priority boarding and lounge access add real convenience.

Pro tip: Many savvy cardholders use both—Amex Cobalt for everyday spending and TD Aeroplan for travel. This strategy maximizes rewards across all categories.
